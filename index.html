About the Authors

The Salish Sea Cadre consists of systems thinkers, practical theorists, and advocates for community-led resilience. With a backgrounds in entrepreneurship, engineering, and sustainable systems design, our work focuses on creating actionable frameworks that empower groups to build autonomous, self-sufficient, and equitable futures.

Crisis Cadres is the culmination of years of research and hands-on experimentation, offering a pragmatic roadmap for anyone who believes that the best way to predict the future is to build it ourselves.
